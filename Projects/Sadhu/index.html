About Me

Sahaya Sheelan Shadrach (born 24 March 1966), known by his stage name Sadhu Kokila, is an Indian comedian, musician, actor, film director, producer, occasional screenwriter and lyricist who works in Kannada cinema. He began his career as a composer before taking to acting in films, appearing mostly in comic roles. He has directed ten Kannada films, the most notable one being Raktha Kanneeru (2003).

Date of Birth:24 March 1966

Place: Banglore , Karnataka

Education: He completed his education at St. Joseph's Indian High School in Bangalore and was later awarded an honorary doctorate by the Gangubai Music and Performing Arts University in 2025.

Career Journey

Early Career: Kokila began his career as a musician after he was taken to musician Kasturi Shankar by his brother..

Breakthrough: As a composer, he is a two-time recipient of the Karnataka State Film Award for Best Music Director, for Rakshasa (2005) and Inthi Ninna Preethiya (2008). As an actor, he has received multiple nominations and awards in the category of Best Comedian in the SIIMA Awards.

Popular Roles & Performances:

  • Yaare Koogadali movie SIIMA Award Best Comedian
  • Yajamana movie IIFA Award Best Comedian
