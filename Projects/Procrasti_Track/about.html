👋 Our story

Built by students who
procrastinated first

ProcrastiTrack started as a 3am panic project the night before a deadline. Now it helps thousands of students stay on track — without the panic.

🎯 Our Mission

Make deadlines feel like levels, not punishments

Traditional task managers are boring. Students ignore them. We asked: What if missing a deadline made Duo sad? What if submitting early gave you double XP? What if your whole class competed on a leaderboard?

The answer: 40% better on-time submission rates in our early trials. Gamification works.
